Electrical Services Design

Every electrical installation must be preceded by good electrical service design, electrical service design is the graphical representation of various electrical loads such as lighting, power, distribution board schematics, fire prevention design, public address system, CCTV installation design, etc. Lighting Installation

What is light? Light is a form of electromagnetic radiation that is part of radiant energy from a hot body which produce the visual sensation on human eyes. Lighting installation means a fixed electrical lighting system in the building ‘luminaire’ means a lighting device, which distributes light from a single lamp or a group of lamps; a luminaire should include control gears if applicable, and all necessary components for fixing and mechanical protection of lamps.

What is the specification of lighting system?

Provide adequate illumination level according to the activity of the area for example bed room, kitchen so on. 2. Provide sufficient uniform distribution of light overall the working plane. A very good uniformity ratio according to European specification is UR ≥ 0.5 3. Avoid glare and hard shadows because these cause fatigue and lack of concentration of persons 4. Discriminate between different colors as well as the degree of color which mean color rendering index What is color rendering index ? This is a system that measure the ability of light source to accurately produce the color of an object. Lighting Scheme 1. Direct lighting scheme: Used in industrial places, workshops and stores. 2. Indirect lighting scheme: Frequently used comfort areas where residents spend more time to relax such as cinema halls and hotels. 3. Semi direct lighting scheme: Used in Domestic areas and offices. 4. Semi indirect lighting scheme: Used in reception areas, interior decoration and painting galleries. 5. General diffusing lighting scheme: Used in areas require to see in three dimensions.
